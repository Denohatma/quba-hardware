About Quba Hardware

Building Kenya's future, one project at a time.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

Quba Hardware was founded with a simple mission: to provide builders, contractors, and homeowners with access to the highest quality construction materials at fair prices.

From our first store in Machakos town, we've grown to serve customers across two locations, building lasting relationships with both our clients and Kenya's most trusted construction brands.

Every product on our shelves is chosen for its reliability and value. We believe that great buildings start with great materials — and that's exactly what we deliver.
