Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

Quba Hardware started in Tetu, Nyeri with a simple mission: to provide builders, contractors, and homeowners with access to the highest quality construction materials at fair prices, no matter the size of the project.

From our first branch in Tetu, we expanded to Machakos town, and our ambition is to be in every county, serving our customers excellently and conveniently, wherever they are building.

We work with projects of all sizes, from small home renovations to major developments, offering flexible approaches that suit each customer. Every product on our shelves is chosen for its reliability and value, because great buildings start with great materials.
